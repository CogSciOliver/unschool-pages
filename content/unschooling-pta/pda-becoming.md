They call us disruptive.  
They call us anarchist.  
They call us difficult.  
They us me bossy or intolerable.   

All these names are labels for people who do not submit to the will of anyone but their own will. Is that so wrong in a system that has been training our humanity out of us and into a Eurocentric pattern of normal not even Europeans can tolerate?

PDA reveals something about human free will many people suppress. What we call PDA may be an unconditioned form of autonomy most systems are designed to override.
