## Empowering Parents

A living collection for empowered parents navigating self-directed families, Persistent Demand for Autonomy (PDA), and the realities of raising humans outside conventional systems. This evolving reading list brings together books, ideas, and [rabbit-hole](/rabbit-holes/) explorations for radical thinkers who question control-based parenting and choose relationship, autonomy, and trust instead. Each selection supports deeper understanding of agency, nervous system awareness, and the lived experience of unschooling—where both parent and child are continually becoming, learning, and redefining what it means to grow together.
