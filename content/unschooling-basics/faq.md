## Common questions

### Do kids still learn core skills?
Yes. Reading, writing, math, communication, and problem-solving still show up, but often through real use instead of imposed sequencing.

### What does a day look like?
A real day can include conversation, cooking, games, reading aloud, projects, errands, internet rabbit holes, and long stretches of child-led exploration.
