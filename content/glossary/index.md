> Not all words mean the same thing in every world.
> This glossary gathers some of the terms, signals, and meanings used across  
> The Unschool Pages and Unschool Discoveries universe.

# Terms:
<div class="line-space-reset"></div>

## Unschooling

Learning through life, curiosity, relationships, and real experiences instead of a fixed school-style system.

## Worldschooling

Learning through travel, culture, place, people, and the wider world.

## Deschooling

The process of letting go of school habits, school fears, and school ways of thinking.

## Self-Directedness

The ability to guide your own life, choices, learning, and growth from within. It is rooted in autonomy, responsibility, adaptability, and trust in one’s own direction.

## Self-Directed Learning

Learning led by interest, need, readiness, and choice.

## The Unschool Pages

The notebook behind the universe, where ideas, scenes, notes, and real-life observations are gathered.

## Unschool Discoveries

The story universe itself, where the adventures, characters, and lived experiences unfold.

## Artifact

A trace, object, note, drawing, recording, or fragment collected along the journey.

## Threshold

The point where one way of seeing ends and another begins.

## The Unschool Pages

The notebook behind the universe, where ideas, field notes, observations, and behind-the-scenes making are gathered.

## The 5 Ease Methodology

Under the umbrella of Encouragement, a way of understanding growth, learning, and living through five unfolding phases of ease, awareness, and becoming.

## Witness

To notice, observe, and honor what is happening without rushing to control or explain it away.

## Living Record

A body of work that preserves life as it is being lived, felt, noticed, and remembered.

## Genetic Memory

The felt sense that some knowledge, instincts, patterns, and stories live deep within us and move across generations.

## Cookie-Cutter System

A one-size-fits-all way of organizing life, learning, and success.

## Compulsory Systems

Systems people are expected to follow whether or not they fit human needs, curiosity, or freedom.

## Threshold

The point where one way of seeing or living ends and another begins.

## Voyage

A journey of discovery, change, and becoming across time, place, and imagination.

## Canon

The core stories, meanings, and truths that shape a universe from within.

## Atmosphere

The feeling, tone, and emotional weather of a world.

## Lifelong Learning

Learning that continues across a whole life, not just during school years.

## Narrator

The voice that guides the audience through the story, the world, or the record.

## Documentarian

One who observes, gathers, records, and shapes real-life moments into lasting work.

## Worldbuilder

One who creates and reveals the people, places, rules, symbols, and feeling of a living universe.
